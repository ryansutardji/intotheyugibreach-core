BATTLE LOG

LEGEND

Outline = team (blue outline = you, red outline = enemy).
Click a unit, then a blue tile to move, then a red-outlined enemy to attack. Click your unit again to skip the attack.
Enemy turn preview: orange dashed tile + line = where an enemy will move. Red tiles = where it will strike this turn — a numbered badge means it hits that tile more than once. Move off a red tile or kill the enemy first to prevent the hit. Gray dashed = an enemy retreating (it won't attack).
Mission objectives: yellow ring = the enemy's priority target — destroying it wins instantly.
Terrain: purple dashed tiles are pits — impassable, and instantly destroy anything knocked into one. Slate dashed tiles are chasms — also impassable, but a knockback into one only deals impact damage and collapses the wall, leaving safe ground behind. Stone-gray tiles are mountains — passable, and halve damage taken by whoever's standing on them. Orange dashed tiles are lava — passable, but burn whoever's standing there for a chunk of damage at the start of their turn. Cyan dashed tiles are ice — passable, and frictionless: a knockback that lands on ice keeps sliding in the same direction until it reaches non-ice ground or hits something that stops it. Every melee hit shoves the target back a tile — into a pit kills it, into a chasm damages and collapses it, into a building damages the building, into another unit damages both. Solid orange tiles are your buildings — impassable, with their own HP bar when damaged. Lose every building and the mission ends in defeat, no matter what the stated objective is or how the rest of your squad is doing.
